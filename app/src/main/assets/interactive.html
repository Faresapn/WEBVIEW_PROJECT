Interactive WebView

You can interact with the native functions of the phone, from the local or remote html!

Vibrate Smartphone

You can operate the phone to vibrate by a button.

WebAppInterface.vibrate(milliseconds);

Show Toast

You can show a toast by passing the message from JS.

WebAppInterface.vibrate('Hello World!');

Play Sound

Play a mp3 file from your app..

WebAppInterface.playSound();

Push Notification

Create a new notification by passing title and message

WebAppInterface.newNotification(title, msg);

Create Snak Bar

Like Toast, you can create a new snak bar

WebAppInterface.snakBar(msg);
